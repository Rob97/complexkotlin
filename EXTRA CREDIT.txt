Seneca the Younger aka Lucius Annaeus Seneca was a Roman Stoic philosopher.
The school of philosophy he is commonly associated with is Stoicism which
is a school of Hellenistic philosophy.In the most simplistic form, the 
Stoicism's school taught that virtue is considered as the highestgood,
that virtue is based on knowledge and that one's happiness and judgement
should be based on behaviors instead of words.
